Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7th 2026

Overview

Indexly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Indexly operates entirely on your local device:

  • All saved messages are stored locally in your browser's storage
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics or tracking is performed

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to save:

  • Your saved ChatGPT messages
  • Tags and organization data
  • Search preferences

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Permissions

Indexly requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect when you're on ChatGPT and inject the save button
  • storage: To save your messages locally on your device

Host Permissions

The extension only works on:

  • https://chat.openai.com/*
  • https://chatgpt.com/*

This is required to inject the save functionality into ChatGPT pages.

Third-Party Services

Indexly does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your saved data at any time by:

  1. Opening the extension popup
  2. Deleting individual messages, or
  3. Uninstalling the extension (which removes all local data)

Children's Privacy

Indexly is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@indexly.app
